# Case Summary: Death at Thornwood Manor

## Case Number: TM-2024-0892

**Victim**: Richard Thornwood, 62 years old
**Occupation**: Wealthy art collector and gallery owner
**Date of Death**: October 15, 2024
**Time of Death**: Estimated between 9:00 PM and 10:00 PM
**Location**: Thornwood Manor, main library

## Circumstances

Richard Thornwood was hosting a small dinner party at his mansion when he was found dead in his private library. The body was discovered by his housekeeper, Margaret Wilson, at approximately 10:15 PM when she went to offer after-dinner drinks.

## Cause of Death

Blunt force trauma to the back of the head. The weapon appears to be a bronze sculpture from the victim's own collection.

## Persons of Interest

Four individuals were present at the manor during the estimated time of death:
1. Alice Chen - Art dealer
2. Bob Martinez - Victim's nephew
3. Carol Thompson - Former business partner
4. David Kim - Recently dismissed personal assistant

## Evidence Collected

- Murder weapon (bronze sculpture)
- Wine glass with lipstick traces
- Torn letter fragments
- Muddy shoes found near garden entrance
- Forensic samples from the scene

## Investigation Status

OPEN - Awaiting analysis and determination of perpetrator
