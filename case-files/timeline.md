# Timeline of Events - October 15, 2024

## Before Dinner

**5:00 PM** - David Kim arrives at the manor, despite being fired last week. Claims he came to collect personal belongings and discuss severance.

**6:00 PM** - Alice Chen arrives. She and Richard have a private conversation in his study for approximately 30 minutes.

**6:30 PM** - Bob Martinez arrives. He appears nervous according to the housekeeper.

**6:45 PM** - Carol Thompson arrives last. She and Richard exchange cold glances but remain civil.

## Dinner (7:00 PM - 8:45 PM)

**7:00 PM** - Dinner begins in the main dining room. All four guests plus Richard present.

**7:30 PM** - Heated discussion about art prices. Alice and Richard argue briefly.

**8:00 PM** - Richard excuses himself to take a phone call. Gone for 15 minutes.

**8:15 PM** - Richard returns, appears agitated. Says something about "matters being settled soon."

**8:45 PM** - Dinner concludes. Richard invites everyone to the parlor for drinks but says he needs to retrieve something from the library first.

## Critical Window (9:00 PM - 10:00 PM)

**9:00 PM** - Richard enters the library alone.

**9:05 PM** - Carol Thompson excuses herself to use the restroom. Gone approximately 20 minutes.

**9:10 PM** - Bob Martinez steps outside to smoke. Claims he walked in the garden.

**9:15 PM** - Alice Chen says she went to her car to retrieve a gift for Richard.

**9:25 PM** - Carol returns to the parlor.

**9:30 PM** - David Kim noticed leaving through the back door by the housekeeper.

**9:35 PM** - Bob returns from the garden.

**9:40 PM** - Alice returns with a small wrapped package.

**10:00 PM** - Housekeeper notes the library door is still closed.

## Discovery

**10:15 PM** - Housekeeper finds Richard dead in the library when she goes to offer drinks.

**10:18 PM** - Police are called.

**10:45 PM** - First officers arrive on scene.
